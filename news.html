PSAU Soaring Eagles Robotics Team Represents Philippines Again at VEX World Championship

The Soaring Eagles Robotics Team from Pampanga State Agricultural University (PSAU) is proudly representing the Philippines for the second consecutive year at the VEX Robotics World Championship 2025 in Dallas, Texas. Competing in the VEX U Math Division, the team joins top global universities in showcasing excellence in engineering and robotics. Their return follows a strong performance at the 2024–2025 VEX “High Stakes” Scrimmage, where they earned the Tournament Champion title. PSAU continues to inspire pride nationwide with their innovation, skill, and dedication to STEM.

PSAU's Johan Dretl Gulang Tops October 2024 Geodetic Engineer Licensure Exam

Pampanga State Agricultural University (PSAU) celebrates a significant achievement as its graduate, Johan Dretl Gulang, secures the top spot in the October 2024 Geodetic Engineer Licensure Examination. The Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) announced that Gulang achieved the highest rating among 650 successful examinees out of 1,343 candidates who took the exam. This accomplishment underscores PSAU's commitment to academic excellence and its role in producing top-tier professionals in the field of geodetic engineering. Congratulations to Engr. Johan Dretl Gulang and the entire PSAU community for this outstanding achievement.
